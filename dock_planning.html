Logic set

for outbound dock_ time for loading= min(( vehicle bag capacity / 300) or (shipment in facility(p0+p1) / 300)). Say this comes out to be 2.5 hrs(next round figure 3 hrs). With this data based and cutoff timing in drhub, dock will be occupied for 3 hours before cutt off departure. Hence if the cut off departure is 5AM. then dock will be occuped in 2-3 | 3-4 | 4-5 hours brackets. here detialed bifurcation can be taken for B2B/ Heavy / B2C

For inbound dock_ time for loading = bags in trip / 300
